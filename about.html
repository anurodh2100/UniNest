About Us

Welcome to UniNest! Our hostel is designed to provide students with a safe, comfortable, and supportive environment. We offer a wide range of amenities including 24/7 water supply, cleaning services, fully furnished rooms, and Wi-Fi connectivity. Our campus is equipped with CCTV for enhanced security, ensuring peace of mind for our residents. Whether you are a single student or part of an exchange program, UniNest is the ideal place to call home during your studies.
